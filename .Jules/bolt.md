## 2026-03-09 - Pre-calculated objects outside the animation tick\n**Learning:** When executing rapid periodic animations (e.g. `setInterval` in `useEffect`), creating objects inside the tick callback allocates memory rapidly causing stutter due to garbage collection, while also repeating operations pointlessly.\n**Action:** Use initialization scopes within `useEffect` to create arrays, objects, and strings needed by the animation once, to optimize the render cycle.
